2. Introduction

In this chapter I will explain for who I have written this document, how to contact me, and lastly about the licenses used in this project.

2.1 Audience

This document is written for developers who just have begun helping with the Evol Online project. However this document might also be helpfull for experienced developers, as it can act as an in depth guidelines reference.

In this document we will mostly explain the art style of Evol Online. With the help of example images we will explain the look and feel of Evol Online. Some of the chapters will have an in depth guide how to create these look and style.

We hope this document might explain some of the questions a developer may have. However this document is currently far from finished, so please expect that there are incomplete texts and mistakes in this document. If anything is not clear, or if you find a mistake in this document, please do not hesitate to contact us at the IRC or the forum.

2.3 Licenses

Licenses are an important part of the project as they tell what you are, and aren't allowed to do.
