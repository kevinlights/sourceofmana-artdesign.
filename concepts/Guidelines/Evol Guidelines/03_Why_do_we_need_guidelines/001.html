2. Why do we need guidelines?

There are several reasons why we need to have guidelines:

  1. Consistent game experience
  2. Higher quality
  3. It helps beginner developers

2.1 Consistent game experience

Guidelines make sure everything looks, feels, and works the same. Without them each developer would invent their own wheel. And every wheel would look different from each other. One might be round, the other square and another triangle. Each wheel would work different too. One wheel has to be pushed, while another has to be pulled. The third wheel may have an engine, and roll on his own.

2.2 Higher quality

Guidelines also make sure there is a quality standard. This makes sure that every piece of art at least has some quality.

2.3 It helps beginner developers

Beginner developers may not know everything about Evol Online. With the help of the guidelines the beginning developers can check whether their piece of art meets the requirements them self and, if needed, fix their piece of art.
